  • My name is Janet Lester and I am a Software Engineer in Kansas City Missouri. I have been in the Information Technology field for over 20 years. I graduated from CMSU with a Bachelors Degree in Computer Information Systems. Most of my career has been spent with one company in the finance and healthcare sector. There, I performed in variouse roles working on projects using Mainframe languages. I've worked with both Agile and Waterfall project methodologies. In 2018, when that company was sold, I found myself in a new job where I felt unfulfilled and no longer challenged. After some soul-searching, knowing I wanted to say in the IT field since my passion was coding and data, I decided to transition my career into Web Development. So I enrolled in Centriq's Full Stack Developer training where I received hands-on training with various web technologies, performed labs and created several projects. I am now looking forward to new opportunites where I can utilize my experience in IT as well as grow my knowledge and skill set acquired in Web Development. I would like to connect with you on LinkedIn where you can find a link to the various projects I've created.
