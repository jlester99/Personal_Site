About Me

Janet Lester

Hello..I am Janet Lester and I am a Software Engineer based in Kansas City, Missouri.
I started my career in the Information Technology field over 20 years ago after graduating with a Bachelors Degree in Computer Information Systems. I spent the majority of my career in the healthcare and finance sector, primarily for one company, using Mainframe technologies. There, I performed various roles in delivering quality and efficient projects using both Agile and Waterfall methodologies. Then, in 2018, when that company was sold, I found myself in a new job where I felt unfulfilled and no longer challenged. I knew it was time for a change, but I also knew I wanted to stay in IT development, where I really enjoyed the critical thinking, data analysis and the logic side. So, I made the decision to quit my job and enrolled in Centriq's Full Stack Web Developer Training.. In this accelerated training, I gained hands-on training performing labs and creating projects, both independently and as a team, including some pair programming. I am now looking forward to new opportunities where I can hone my new skillset as a Web Developer, continue to learn new technologies, as well as leverage my vast amount of IT experience to be mutually beneficial. Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn and to browse My Portfolio page here which showcases the projects and labs (both required and ones I've done on the side) as a Full Stack Developer.
